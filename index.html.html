Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a landing page and a website?

A landing page is a single webpage designed for a specific purpose, usually to capture leads or promote a single product/service. It's focused on one call-to-action.

A website consists of multiple pages (like Home, About, Services, Contact) and provides more comprehensive information about your business. Websites are better for businesses that need to share more detailed information with visitors.

What's the difference between static and dynamic websites?

A static website displays the same content to every visitor. The content doesn't change unless manually updated by a web developer. It's like a digital brochure with fixed information.

A dynamic website can display different content based on user interactions or database information. It allows for features like user accounts, online shopping, content management systems, and real-time updates. Dynamic websites are essential for e-commerce and businesses that need frequently updated content.

Do I need e-commerce functionality?

You need e-commerce functionality if you want to sell products or services directly through your website. Our Business and Enterprise packages include e-commerce capabilities that allow you to list products, accept payments online, and manage inventory.

If you're just looking to showcase your business without selling online, our Starter or Basic packages would be more suitable.

What domains are included with each package?

Our Starter package includes a .ct.ws domain, which is a cost-effective option for new businesses.

The Basic package includes either a .com OR a .co.za domain of your choice.

The Business and Enterprise packages include BOTH a .com AND a .co.za domain, giving you more professional presence and better protection of your brand online.
